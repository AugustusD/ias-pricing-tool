# IAS Dealer Pricing Tool - Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Industrial Precision
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Industrial Modernism meets Corporate Precision
**Core Principles:** Clean data hierarchy, no-nonsense utility, premium metal aesthetic
**Color Philosophy:** Gold (#B69A5A) as the primary accent on black headers; warm grey surfaces; white for data areas; #f4ce47 for interactive highlights and badges
**Layout Paradigm:** Persistent left sidebar for category navigation (like a physical binder), main content area with tabular product listings, fixed right panel for order summary
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal slash motif from the logo repeated in section dividers; heavy black header bar with gold text; monospaced part codes
**Interaction Philosophy:** Click-to-add with instant quantity controls; order panel slides in from right
**Animation:** Subtle fade-ins for product rows; smooth counter animations on quantity changes
**Typography System:** Helvetica Neue Bold for headers/labels; Helvetica for body; monospace for part codes
</text>

## Approach 3: Premium Catalog (CHOSEN)
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Premium B2B Catalog / Trade Tool
**Core Principles:** Authoritative brand presence, scannable product data, efficient ordering workflow
**Color Philosophy:** Black (#000) header with #B69A5A gold brand elements; off-white (#F8F7F5) background for warmth; dark grey (#2D2D2D) for text; #f4ce47 for CTAs and quantity badges; white cards for product rows
**Layout Paradigm:** Fixed top header with logo + order summary badge; left sidebar with collapsible category tree; main content with product sub-tabs; sticky bottom order bar on mobile
**Signature Elements:** Gold left border on active sidebar items; diagonal slash divider in header (echoing logo); part code in monospace; "NET" badge for net-priced items
**Interaction Philosophy:** Single-click quantity increment with +/- controls; items animate into order panel; search/filter within categories
**Animation:** Smooth sidebar transitions; quantity badge pulse on add; order panel slide-in
**Typography System:** Helvetica Bold (700) for category headers; Helvetica Medium (500) for product names; Helvetica Regular (400) for descriptions; Courier New / monospace for part codes
</text>

</response>

## CHOSEN: Approach 3 - Premium B2B Catalog

**Design philosophy:** A premium trade catalog tool that feels authoritative and efficient. The IAS brand colors (black, gold #B69A5A, white, grey, with #f4ce47 highlights) create a professional, industrial aesthetic. The layout mirrors the workbook structure with tabs, making it intuitive for existing users. The ordering workflow is streamlined with instant feedback.
